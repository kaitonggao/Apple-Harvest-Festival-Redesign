History

______________________

Since 1982, the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival hosts apples, baked goods, family entertainment, games, prizes, live entertainment and more. Over 100 talented artists, crafters, bakers, and makers come together for the Apple Harvest Craft Fair!

Find creative, unique works from clothing to woodworking, ceramics to paintings, fudge to essential oils and beyond throughout the festival. The festival is held in the Ithaca Commons, a few streets that are full of restaurants and shops.

TIPS

Useful tips for your first apple fest!

It is generally a good idea to attend the festival early to avoid crowds

In case of rain, the festival will still occur; shelter can be found underneath the store covers.

Some stalls may only accept cash as payment.

Prices of food items range anywhere from $5 - $20+.

Crafts range from $15 - $100+
